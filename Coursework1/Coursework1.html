Synopsis

Henry Jones lives in a Hawaiian paradise with the company of endless women with no strings attached. This is until he meets Anna Whitmore. Both Henry and Anna enjoy the company of each other and feel the start of a serious relationship occurring. Approaching Anna the next day, Henry is confused when Anna fails to recognize him. This is the moment Henry discovers that Anna actually suffers from short term memory loss and can't remember each individual day. Henry won't let this stop him and is prepared to make her fall in love with him all over again, each and every day.
